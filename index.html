ABOUT THE MOVIE

The movie is an interesting one. it is called underwater, it is basically a horror and an action film at the same time, it is based on water as in the title of the film and unknown speies that feed on people.

Underwater is all about a group of workers on a drilling facility at the bottom of the ocean who encounter hostile creatures after an earthquake destroys the facility.In the movie Norah had to sacrifies herself to save her best friend Emily and her boyfriend Paul.A Sallow but satisfying creature feature that splits the difference between classic disasters movies and lovecraftian horror.
